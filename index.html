Reconstruction of Carla Town10HD with simulator integration

Recreating Carla Town10HD worked as a development environment to make a proof-of-concept for integrating gaussian rendering with vehicle dynamics from Carla.

We can also run the integration with rendering turned off in Carla to increase performance in SIBR-viewer.

Reconstruction of Trip090 - Real-world data

This video showcases a reconstruction of Trip090, with a custom Carla map created from OpenStreetMap data and aligned through geo-referencing with our gaussian model.
