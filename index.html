Synthetic Data

SIBR-Carla integration of Carla Town10HD

Recreating Carla Town10HD worked as a development environment to make a proof-of-concept for integrating gaussian rendering with vehicle dynamics from Carla.

We can also run the integration with rendering turned off in Carla to increase performance in SIBR-viewer.
