Synthetic Data

SIBR-Carla integration of Carla Town10HD

Reconstructing Carla Town10HD was an excellent way to develop the method, by having full control over the environment to reconstruct we could easily generate input data, compare with ground truth and test out methodologies before moving to real-world data.

We can also run the integration with rendering turned off in Carla to increase performance in SIBR-viewer.
